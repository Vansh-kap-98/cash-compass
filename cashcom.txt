CASH COMPASS--
Features--
	--Interactive Financial Avatar: A character that visually reacts to spending habits (gains "energy" on savings, looks "stressed" on overspending).

	--3D Wealth Visualization: An interactive 3D terrain/map using Three.js to represent budget categories instead of traditional flat charts.

	--Burn-Rate Forecaster: Predictive AI that calculates exactly which date the user will hit $0 based on their current 7-day spending average.

	--One-Tap Quick Entry: A high-speed, gesture-based interface to log transactions in under 3 seconds.

	--"Party Mode" (Group Split): Shared temporary lobbies for roommates or trips to automatically calculate and balance group debts.

	--Sub-Stash Micro Goals: A "round-up" tool that sends digital spare change into specific savings buckets for high-priority student purchases.

	--Smart Limit Alerts: Non-intrusive, "welcoming" notifications that trigger when a user approaches 80% of a specific category limit.

	--Dynamic Transaction History: A fluid, animated timeline of all financial activities with search and filter capabilities.
